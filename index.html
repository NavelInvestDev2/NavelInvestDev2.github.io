About

It's all about openness

Investors traditionally have funded Startups the same way since the 1980s: go to networking events, do the due diligence process and hopefully, get a nice return in about 3 to 5 years time.

At Navel, we seek to change that by creating an open-ended community where founders and investors can craft together solutions that fit their needs. This way, we believe we help democratize access to capital for everyone.
